Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we use TikTok data in connection with our internal, self-hosted Postiz social media publishing workflow.

Information We Access

When an authorized TikTok account owner connects their account, we may access basic profile information such as open ID, display name, username, avatar, and account statistics when permitted by TikTok scopes. We may also use content publishing permissions to upload and publish approved videos or photo posts to the authorized TikTok account.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to identify the connected TikTok account in the Postiz dashboard, publish content approved by our internal team, and maintain internal publishing records.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, resell, scrape, or share TikTok user data with third parties. We do not build user profiles or analytics products from TikTok data.

Data Retention

Publishing records and authorization data are retained only as needed to operate the internal publishing workflow. Access can be removed by disconnecting the TikTok account from Postiz or revoking the app in TikTok account settings.

Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the operator of this internal publishing workflow.
